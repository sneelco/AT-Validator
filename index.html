Aerobic Threshold Validator

The 60-minute heart-rate drift test: hold a steady effort at your suspected aerobic-threshold heart rate — if HR rises more than 5% from where it started, the effort was above threshold. Everything runs in your browser; no data leaves this page.

Drop a Garmin .fit or .zip file here, or a timestamp,heartrate .csv

Garmin Connect → activity → gear icon → “Export Original” — the downloaded zip works as-is
